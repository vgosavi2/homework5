Altair Visualizations – UIUC IS445

Chart 1: Average Square Footage by County

This bar chart shows the average building square footage grouped by county. The x-axis is categorical (ordinal) and represents counties, while the y-axis displays the mean square footage (quantitative). I chose a vertical bar chart because it clearly distinguishes the differences in average size. Tooltip interactivity helps view exact values. I applied simple preprocessing by trimming whitespace in column names.

Chart 2: Square Footage Trends Over Time by Usage Type

This line chart shows how average square footage has changed over time (binned into 5-year intervals) for the top 5 most common building usage types. I used line marks with color encoding by usage type and interactive dropdown filtering. The y-axis uses symlog scale to handle outliers. I transformed the year into 5-year bins and dropped rows with missing values.

Interactivity was added through a dropdown selection which allows viewers to isolate and emphasize one usage line at a time—improving clarity and engagement.
